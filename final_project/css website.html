Butterflies Paradise

Butterflies are colorful, delicate insects known for their beautiful wings and graceful flight. They play an important role as pollinators, helping plants reproduce while feeding on nectar. Their life cycle includes four stages: egg, caterpillar, pupa, and adult butterfly. In this website, I will show you facts and different kinds of butterflies, their habitats, and how to create a butterfly-friendly garden to help conserve these amazing creatures.
